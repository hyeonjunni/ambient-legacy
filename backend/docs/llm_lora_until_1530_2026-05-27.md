Output:

```text
기록에는 2018년 여름 광안리 해변 산책이 남아 있으며, 저녁 무렵 바람이 시원했다는 내용만 있습니다. 부산 여행의 정확한 날짜와 식당 이름은 기록에서 확인되지 않습니다.
```

This confirms the Gemma E2B best-100 adapter loads and preserves the intended "do not invent unsupported facts" behavior in this smoke case.

## Recommendation

Use Gemma E2B as the main next candidate, but do not judge quality from this run. The important result is that the local MLX LoRA path works.

Next practical step:

1. Expand SFT data from 6 rows to at least 50-100 rows.
2. Keep a real validation split with at least 10-20 rows.
3. Start with Gemma E2B, `num_layers=4`, `learning_rate=5e-6`.
4. Evaluate checkpoints every 25-50 steps and stop early around the best validation checkpoint.
5. Keep EXAONE as a speed/latency comparison, but Gemma remains the safer primary direction.
